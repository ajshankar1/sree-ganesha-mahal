Our Heritage

A Venue Born from Devotion

✦

Founded in 2009, IGS Convention Centre was established with a vision to provide Tirunelveli's families with a magnificent yet affordable venue for their most sacred celebrations. Located in the heart of Palayamkottai, we have grown to become one of Tirunelveli's most trusted event venues.

Over 15 years, we have expanded from a single hall to a grand multi-hall convention complex — featuring four beautifully named halls: Shree Ganesha Mahal, Kaveri Hall, Vaigai Hall, and Thamirabarani Hall — each named after sacred rivers and deities of Tamil Nadu.

We understand that your celebration is not just an event — it is a sacred moment that will be remembered for generations. That is why every detail at IGS Convention Centre is designed to make your event truly extraordinary.
