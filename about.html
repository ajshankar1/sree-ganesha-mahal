Our Heritage

A Venue Born from Devotion

✦

Founded in 2009 by the Pillai family, Sree Ganesha Mahal was established with a vision to provide Tirunelveli's families with a magnificent yet affordable venue for their most sacred celebrations. Named after the auspicious Lord Ganesha — the remover of obstacles — our mahal has been a blessing for thousands of families.

Over 15 years, we have grown from a modest 200-capacity hall to a grand 700-capacity air-conditioned venue with state-of-the-art facilities. Yet our core values remain unchanged: impeccable service, honest pricing, and genuine care for every family we serve.

We understand that your wedding is not just an event — it is a sacred moment that will be remembered for generations. That is why every detail at Sree Ganesha Mahal is designed to make your celebration truly extraordinary.
