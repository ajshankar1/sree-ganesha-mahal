Everything Under One Roof

✦

Sree Ganesha Mahal is equipped with everything your event needs — no compromise, no shortcuts.

Air Conditioned Hall

The entire main hall and dining area are fully air-conditioned with industrial-grade units. Backup AC system ensures uninterrupted comfort throughout your event, even during peak summer.

Grand Dining Hall

Separate dining hall accommodating 300 guests at a time. Equipped with stainless steel serving counters, water stations, and proper ventilation for comfortable dining.

Ample Parking

Secure, organized parking for 200 cars and 300 two-wheelers. Supervised by trained security personnel throughout your event. CCTV coverage for complete safety.

Luxury Bridal Suite

An elegantly furnished bridal room with full-length mirror, makeup area, comfortable seating, and private attached bathroom. The bride's sanctuary for the perfect preparation.

Guest Rooms

5 well-furnished AC guest rooms for the wedding party and close family members. Each room includes clean linen, hot water, and basic amenities. Available at ₹2,000 per room.

Generator Backup

Full-capacity generator activates within seconds of any power disruption. Covers all AC units, lights, sound systems, and kitchen equipment — ensuring zero interruption.

Decoration Area

Dedicated area for decorators to set up floral arrangements, lighting rigs, and stage decorations in advance. Venue access from 6 AM for setup convenience.

Catering Kitchen

Professional catering space with cooking stations, utensil storage, large water supply, and proper ventilation. Supports both in-house catering and outside caterers.
