Everything Under One Roof

✦

IGS Convention Centre is equipped with everything your event needs — no compromise, no shortcuts.

Fully Air-Conditioned Halls

All four halls — Shree Ganesha Mahal, Kaveri, Vaigai and Thamirabarani — are fully air-conditioned with industrial-grade units. Backup AC ensures uninterrupted comfort even during peak Tamil Nadu summers.

Dining Hall — 180 Guests

Spacious dining hall accommodating 180 guests at a time. Equipped with stainless steel serving counters, water stations, and proper ventilation. Supports both vegetarian and non-vegetarian menus.

Traditional Wood Stove Kitchen

Our signature wood stove kitchen brings authentic South Indian flavours to your wedding feast. Experienced cooks prepare traditional multi-course meals the old-fashioned way — the kind guests remember for years.

Veg & Non-Veg Catering

Full in-house catering for both vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals. Classic South Indian wedding menus, multi-course feasts, and custom menus crafted by experienced cooks. Outside caterers also welcome.

15 AC Guest Rooms

15 fully air-conditioned guest rooms for the wedding party and out-of-town family members. Each room features clean linen, hot water, modern amenities, and 24/7 housekeeping service.

Common Parking

Large common parking area for cars and two-wheelers. Supervised by trained security personnel throughout your event with complete CCTV surveillance for vehicle and guest safety.

Luxury Bridal Suite

Elegantly furnished bridal suite with full-length mirror, professional makeup area, comfortable seating, and private attached bathroom. The bride's personal sanctuary for flawless preparation.

Generator Backup

Full-capacity generator activates within seconds of any power disruption. Covers all AC units, lighting, sound systems and kitchen equipment across all four halls — zero interruption guaranteed.

Decoration Setup Area

Dedicated area for decorators to set up floral arrangements, lighting rigs, and stage decorations in advance. Venue access from 6 AM so your decorator can work without rush.

24/7 Security & CCTV

Round-the-clock security personnel and comprehensive CCTV surveillance across all halls, parking areas, corridors and common spaces. Your guests and belongings are always in safe hands.
