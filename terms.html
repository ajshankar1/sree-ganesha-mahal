Welcome to IGS Convention Centre. By making a booking or enquiry with us, you agree to the following terms and conditions. Please read them carefully.

1. Booking & Confirmation

  • A booking is confirmed only upon receipt of the advance payment of minimum ₹25,000.
  • The booking date and hall are reserved exclusively for the confirmed event only after advance payment.
  • Verbal commitments or enquiries do not constitute a confirmed booking.
  • A signed booking agreement and receipt will be issued upon confirmation.

2. Payment Terms

  • Advance payment: Minimum ₹25,000 to confirm booking (adjusted in final bill).
  • Balance payment must be cleared at least 7 days before the event date.
  • Accepted modes: Cash, UPI (GPay/PhonePe/Paytm), NEFT/RTGS, Cheque.
  • Receipts will be issued for all payments made.
  • GST will be applicable as per prevailing government norms.

3. Cancellation Policy

  • 60 or more days before event: 80% refund of advance paid.
  • 30–60 days before event: 50% refund of advance paid.
  • 15–30 days before event: 25% refund of advance paid.
  • Less than 15 days before event: No refund.
  • Refunds will be processed within 7–10 working days.

4. Date Change Policy

  • One date change is permitted subject to hall availability.
  • A rescheduling fee of ₹5,000 applies for date changes.
  • Date change requests must be made at least 30 days in advance.

5. Hall Usage

  • The hall must be vacated by the agreed end time. Extension charges may apply.
  • The client is responsible for any damage to the venue, furniture, or equipment during the event.
  • Outside food and catering is allowed with prior approval and nominal kitchen usage charges.
  • Decorators may access the venue from 6 AM on the event day with prior coordination.
  • Music and sound must comply with local noise regulations and curfew timings.

6. Liability

  • IGS Convention Centre is not liable for any loss, damage, or injury to guests or belongings beyond reasonable care.
  • We are not responsible for delays or cancellations caused by circumstances beyond our control (force majeure).

7. Contact for Queries
