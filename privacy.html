Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal information.

HomePrivacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

IGS Convention Centre ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, phone number, email address when you submit an enquiry or booking form.
  • Event details such as date, type of event, guest count, and hall preference.
  • Payment details (processed securely — we do not store card details).
  • Usage data when you visit our website (browser type, pages visited, time spent).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process your booking enquiry and confirm hall availability.
  • To contact you about your event requirements and booking.
  • To send event reminders and follow-up communications.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. WhatsApp & Phone Communication

When you contact us via WhatsApp or phone, your number is stored for the purpose of managing your booking. We may send event confirmations, reminders, and follow-ups via WhatsApp.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. Please contact us if you have concerns about data security.

5. Your Rights

  • You may request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • You may request correction or deletion of your data by contacting us.
  • You may opt out of marketing communications at any time.

6. Contact Us

For privacy-related queries, contact us at +91 98416 08160 or [email protected].
