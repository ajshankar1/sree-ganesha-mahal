About The Venue

A Sacred Space for Life's Greatest Moments

✦

Nestled in the cultural heart of Tirunelveli, IGS Convention Centre has been the venue of choice for grand weddings, sacred ceremonies, and memorable celebrations for over 15 years. Our complex blends the elegance of traditional South Indian architecture with modern amenities.

With four fully air-conditioned halls, 15 AC guest rooms, a 180-seat dining hall with traditional wood stove kitchen, and veg & non-veg catering — we transform your vision into a magnificent reality.
