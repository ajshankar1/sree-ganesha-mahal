About The Venue

A Sacred Space for Life's Greatest Moments

✦

Nestled in the cultural heart of Tirunelveli, Sree Ganesha Mahal has been the venue of choice for grand weddings, sacred ceremonies, and memorable celebrations for over 15 years. Our mandapam blends the elegance of traditional South Indian architecture with modern amenities.

With a sprawling air-conditioned hall that accommodates up to 900 guests, dedicated dining space, and meticulous attention to every detail, we transform your vision into a magnificent reality.
